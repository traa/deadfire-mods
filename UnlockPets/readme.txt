Unlock Pets Mod

brought to you by DRE 8472

Unlocks the pet slot on all companions, sidekicks and hired adventurers.  

IMPORTANT ONLY FOR FIRST TIME USE:
-If using an existing save; switch out the active party.  You can do this by accessing the Party Management menu when on your ship or at an inn. When you bring the character back into your party the slot will be unlocked.  

COMPATIBILITY:
-Designed on game version 5.0.0.0040
-Does not prevent achievements!

INSTALLATION:
Add UnlockPets folder to your Override directory.

REMOVAL:
Remove the UnlockPets folder from your Override directory or disable in the in-game Mod Manager.
